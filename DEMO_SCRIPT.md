# StadiumPulse AI — 75-second Demo Script

**0–10 sec — Landing**

“Meet StadiumPulse AI: one live stadium pulse, transformed into three role-specific copilots for FIFA World Cup 2026. I can switch instantly between Fan, Volunteer and Organizer.”

**10–30 sec — Fan Copilot**

“First, the Fan Copilot. It understands the stadium’s actual gates, accessibility routes and amenities, and combines that with the live gate congestion panel. I’ll ask how to reach an accessible entrance. The answer is grounded in venue data, not generic chat. I can also set an arrival time and interests to generate a personalized match-day plan.”

**30–48 sec — Volunteer Copilot**

“Next, volunteers receive an AI shift briefing made from the active incident feed, in their selected language. This Quick Translate feature gives staff a natural translation and pronunciation guide, helping them support international fans face-to-face.”

**48–70 sec — Organizer Dashboard**

“Finally, organizers can simply ask the stadium what action to take. Gemini reasons over the live simulated gates, zones, incidents and occupancy. The density grid and trend chart create shared situational awareness, while threshold alerts recommend reroutes before queues become unsafe.”

**70–75 sec — Close**

“StadiumPulse AI turns tournament operations from reactive management into a calm, coordinated, fan-first experience.”
